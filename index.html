Rihanna over LA? - 20 Oct 2013
Rihanna apparently wants to "cut ties" with her LA home.
The star's Pacific Palisades mansion has been targeted by intruders and was allegedly invaded twice since the end of September. A removals firm has packed up her belongings as she performs her Diamonds World Tour in South Africa and Rihanna is reportedly wary about returning to the house.

"Even though she wasn't there when it happened, she doesn't feel safe in the house anymore," a source admitted to British magazine Star.
"Rihanna is really scared. She'd rather just cut ties with the house and move on."
Scherzinger 'confused by ex' - 17 Nov 2013
Nicole Scherzinger is apparently frustrated with Lewis Hamilton "blowing hot and cold".

The former Pussycat Doll and the Formula One star started dating in 2007, but broke up and reunited several times before calling things off for good in July this year.
Since then they've been spotted meeting up for "crisis talks" and it was recently reported Lewis promised Nicole children if she takes him back.
Now it seems the sports star has changed his mind about getting back together, leaving the 35-year-old in a confusing position.
